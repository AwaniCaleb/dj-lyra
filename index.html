ABOUT LYRA

Born in Port Harcourt, I found peace early on in radio shows and my humble MP3 player. My passion for music grew as I embraced diverse sounds, especially the artistry of Nigerian musicians.

My DJ journey began unexpectedly in my first year at university when a chance encounter with a DJ set changed everything. With a mentor's help, I honed my skills and soon found myself playing at parties and clubs.

I blend global trends with Amapiano and Afrobeats to create unforgettable moments on any stage. For me, DJing isn't just about music—it's about spreading joy and connecting with my audience.
